Meet Woo

Some conversations shape a lifetime

The Woo Cycle is a beautifully illustrated picture book that helps children understand their bodies through story, imagination and gentle conversation.

Choose your copy → Our Story
? ?

Rather than treating menstrual health like a science lesson, The Woo Cycle introduces body literacy in a way that feels calm, accessible and age appropriate. It invites children to ask questions, encourages adults to answer with confidence, and creates space for empathy, curiosity and connection.

Perfect for families, educators, libraries and anyone who believes every child deserves to grow up understanding their body without shame.
